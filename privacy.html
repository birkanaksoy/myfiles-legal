Last updated: June 29, 2026

My Files ("the app") is a browser and download manager. We care about your privacy and designed the app to collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what happens with your data.

What we do NOT do

  • No account. You don't need to sign up. We do not collect your name, email, or other personal identifiers to use the app.
  • No tracking, no ads. We do not track you and do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • Your files stay on your device. Downloaded files and thumbnails are stored only on your device, inside the app's private storage. They are not uploaded to us.

Data processed by our server

When you ask the app to download from a web page (rather than a direct file link), the app sends the link (URL) you provided to our processing server so it can resolve the media and deliver it to your device.

  • The link is used only to fulfill your request. The video/file content is not stored or archived on our server — it is processed transiently and streamed to your device.
  • To prevent abuse, temporary technical logs (such as IP address and timestamp) may be kept for a short period. These are not used for marketing or profiling.

Device verification (App Attest)

To confirm that requests come from a genuine copy of the app, we use Apple's App Attest service. This does not collect personal data; it generates an anonymous cryptographic key per device.

Photos permission

If you choose to save a downloaded video or image to your Photos library, the app asks for permission to add to Photos. This permission is used only for that action.

Third-party sites

The files you download come from third-party websites and platforms, which have their own privacy policies. We process content only at your request and do not control those sites.

Deleting your data

You can delete all local data at any time from Settings → Clear all files. Deleting the app also removes all local data.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available on this page.
